TimeLine

(Third header)

It all started from the beginnning when mankind was desperate to find a way to store information in a way other than to write it down by hand. Thus, computer storage was invented as a substitute. In the 21st century, USBs, Hard drives, SSDs are the dominating devices that are usually thought of when saying the term "computer storage".

  • Right after the 17th century, devices like punch cards, punched tape, magnetic drums were invented
  • In 1956, IBM took a leap and invented the hard disk. The first ones had a size up to 5 MB(megabytes) which was more than enough back in the day
  • During the years 1950 and 1980, technology advanced forward and new devices were made to store information, one of those being the floppy disk
    • considered as a revolutionery device, it was a lot cheaper than the hard drives and it was a lot more flexible. However it didnt hold as much memory
  • CDs were invented at the begining of the 1980s but were used more in the 1990s.
  • in the 1990s flash memory was also developed such as memory sticks, flash cards etc
  • in the 2000s better versions of Hard drives, SSDs, etc have been introduced
