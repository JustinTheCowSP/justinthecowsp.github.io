Technology

- The top manufacturers for hard disk drives are Western Digital and Seagate
- Hard disks are usually made up of platters, read/write head which are both contained in a case
- Hard drives operate by magnetizing ferrous grains on film on a metal platter
Metal platter spins Head checks/changes rotation of metal grains with magnet as it passes Allows head to access all parts of platter Rotation of metal magnetic field of grains represents 1’s and 0’s Can be multiple platters for more storage Flash memory - SK hynix, Samsung Made up of transistors Special transistors that can keep a charge after circuit is broken Made from silicon Has source and drain regions with control gate and floating gate between
